ABOUT ME

Hi There! I'm Keerthivasna Gurusami or KG for ease of pronounciation. I am a Mechanical Design Engineer with a knack for building better better machines and a mild obsession with making things work smarter stronger and occasionally prettier

After earning my Master’s in Mechanical Engineering from the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign, I dove headfirst into the world of product design, system integration, and FEA. From material handling equipment in Minnesota to self-propelled agricultural sprayers in Indiana, I’ve designed, analyzed, and optimized components that survive real-world abuse and still look good on a CAD screen.

Along the way, I’ve led off-road racing teams, built heat exchangers, optimized battery cooling systems, and automated those pesky BOM exports with a dash of VBA wizardry. I believe good engineering is part creativity, part precision, and a healthy dose of curiosity.

When I'm not chasing stress plots or debating bolt sizes, you’ll probably find me tinkering with new ideas, or perfecting my off-side cover drive.
